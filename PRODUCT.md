# Product

## Register

**Product** (default). Design serves the task: log a day, pick a card later, share.

The marketing landing (`/`) may lean slightly more expressive, but tokens, density, and components stay product-first so `/app` and auth never feel like a different site.

## Users & Purpose

**Who:** Solo creators, students, and indie builders who want a lightweight daily log and a pretty shareable card — not a heavy habit tracker.

**Context:** End of day on phone or laptop; 30–90 seconds to write free text, later export a portrait card for Stories / X / Discord.

**Primary jobs:**

1. Sign in quickly  
2. Write today’s progress (free text)  
3. (Later) Choose a template card and share  

## Brand personality

Three words: **intimate · crafted · clear**

- Intimate: a private notebook energy, not a corporate SaaS dashboard shout  
- Crafted: templates (chibi, pixel, minimal) feel intentional, not stock  
- Clear: product UI disappears into the task — high contrast, no noise  

## References (named)

- **Linear** — restrained surface, one calm accent, dense but readable app chrome  
- **Notion** — pure white content surface, soft borders, task-first density  
- **Day One / paper journals** — date-led journaling mental model (not visual clone)  

## Anti-references

- Tropical / lagoon / “island starter” aesthetic  
- Cream/sand body backgrounds with teal glass cards  
- Purple gradient heroes, glassmorphism stacks, metric-stat SaaS landing grids  
- Side-stripe cards, gradient text, decorative CSS grids  
- Over-rounded (32px+) panels and dual border+huge-shadow ghost cards  

## Voice

Short, plain, human. Prefer “Write today” over “Boost your productivity.” Errors are specific and calm.

## Accessibility

- WCAG AA minimum (body text ≥ 4.5:1; prefer near 7:1 on primary surfaces)  
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`  
- Visible focus rings on all interactive controls  
- Don’t rely on color alone for state  

## Strategic design principles

1. **Surface stays pure** — white / near-black; brand lives in primary + accent, not tinted page backgrounds.  
2. **One primary action per view** — e.g. “Log today” on dashboard.  
3. **Cards are content, not chrome** — share cards get personality; app chrome stays quiet.  
4. **Consistency over novelty** — same button, input, and radius language everywhere.  
5. **Motion only for state** — 150–250ms ease-out; no page-load choreography.
